I'm currently a research software engineer at Stanford University, where I'm part of the Stanford Center for Biomedical Informatics Research (BMIR). I primarily work on the CEDAR Project, an exciting initiative addressed to overcome the impediments to creating high-quality metadata.

Before coming to the US I worked as a researcher and software engineer at the University of A Coruña (Spain), where I obtained my PhD in Artificial Intelligence. I have also taught several college courses related to Web Application development and Semantic Web technologies.
